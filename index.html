Praxis 5005 Science Diagnostic

Identify your strengths and target your study time

This diagnostic covers the three content domains of the Praxis 5005 Elementary Education: Science subtest — Earth Science, Life Science, and Physical Science — including questions that test scientific inquiry and process skills.

You'll answer 35 questions in about 30 minutes. Afterward, you'll receive a personalized report showing your proficiency level, domain strengths, and specific topics to review.

You can retake the diagnostic as many times as you would like to track your improvement. There is a large bank of questions, so each test will be largely unique.

Your E-Number is anonymized (hashed) before being stored. It is only used to track your progress across retakes and to help your instructor understand how well this tool supports learning.
